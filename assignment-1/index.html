About the Song

“No. 1 Party Anthem” is a slow-burning, late-night track that captures the quiet confidence and emotional vulnerability hidden beneath the surface of nightlife bravado.

Unlike the high-energy tracks on AM, this song leans into introspection, portraying a narrator caught between romantic hope and social self-awareness.
